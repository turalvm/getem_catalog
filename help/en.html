GETEM Catalog Pro

A screen‑reader friendly desktop application to easily search and download audiobooks and described movies from the GETEM digital library.

⚙️ Basic Usage

  • First launch: choose your language and enter your GETEM credentials once via Account / Login menu.
  • Switch between Books / Movies / Favorites tabs.
  • Use the search box and genre filter to narrow results.
  • Select a work, then click Download ZIP or press Ctrl+E to start downloading.
  • Download progress is shown on the progress bar and announced by the screen reader in real time.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ↓/↑ – navigate the list
  • Enter – open the work's GETEM page
  • Ctrl+E – download selected work
  • Ctrl+D – add/remove from favorites
  • Ctrl+K – show description (summary) of the selected work
  • Ctrl+B – switch to book catalog, Ctrl+M – movie catalog, Ctrl+F – favorites list
  • Esc – stop ongoing download
  • F1 – open this help page

📊 Sorting

Right‑click → Sort by on the list gives you these options:

  • Default (Original order) – the natural order from GETEM
  • By title (A‑Z / Z‑A)
  • By format (A‑Z / Z‑A) – e.g. “human voice mp3”, “described”
  • By view count (Low‑High / High‑Low)

Your choice is applied immediately and announced to the screen reader.

🔊 Sound Settings

Options → Disable sound turns off all notification sounds temporarily. You can re‑enable it from the same menu. Your preference is saved.

🗂️ Data Management

  • Options → Clear all data resets all catalog files, favorites, settings and browser session. The program restarts with sounds enabled and your language preference preserved.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Download limit: GETEM may ask for a waiting period between consecutive downloads. The program detects this and tells you how many seconds to wait. Try again after that time.
  • Wrong login: If your username or password is incorrect, the program detects the error message on the page and reports it without starting the download. Check your credentials.
  • Internet connection: Required for catalog updates.
🔒 Privacy: All your data is stored locally on your computer.
